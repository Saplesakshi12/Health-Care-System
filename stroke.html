1):-clopidogrel (Plavix):-

Clopidogrel is used to prevent heart attack and stroke

Advantage:-

Clopidogrel is used to prevent blood clots if you have chest pain, peripheral artery disease (poor circulation in your legs), a heart attack, or stroke. .

Dosage:-

Typical starting dosage: 300 mg, taken one time. Starting treatment without a loading dose will delay effects by several days. Maintenance dosage: 75 mg, taken once per day.

Side effect:-

bleeding
itchy skin
