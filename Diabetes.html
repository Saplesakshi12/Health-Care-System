Diabetes Mellitus

Generic and Trade Names of Drugs for Treatment of Diabetes Mellitus

1):-Metformin:-

Metformin is an oral medication that lowers blood glucose (sugar) by influencing the body's sensitivity to insulin and is used for treating type 2 diabetes.

Advantage:-

1) Metformin is a biguanide drug that increases the sensitivity of the bodyâ€™s cells to insulin.

2) It also decreases the amount of glucose produced by the liver.

Dosage:-

in adults, metformin (immediate release) usually is begun at a dose of 500 mg twice a day or 850 mg once daily.

Side effect:-

Possible side effects of metformin include nausea and diarrhea.

2)Thiazolidinediones (Pioglitazone):-

Thiazolidinedione drugs lower blood glucose by increasing the sensitivity of the cells to insulin (improving target cell response to insulin). Examples include pioglitazone (Actos) and rosiglitazone (Avandia)

Advantage:-

1)Pioglitazone is an oral drug that reduces the amount of glucose (sugar) in the blood.

2)It is in a class of anti-diabetic drugs called thiazolidinediones that are used in the treatment of type 2 diabetes.

Dosage:-

Actos is prescribed once daily in doses ranging from 15 to 45 mg.

Side effect:-

Upper respiratory tract infection
Headache
Sinusitis
Muscle aches
Tooth disorders
