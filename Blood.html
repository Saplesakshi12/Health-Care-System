Metformin is an oral medication that lowers blood glucose (sugar) by influencing the body's sensitivity to insulin and is used for treating type 2 diabetes.

Advantage:-

1) Diuretics increase urination which reduces sodium and fluid in the body.

2) That can help lower blood pressure because it lowers blood volume.

3) Mild hypertension can sometimes be treated using diuretics alone, although they are more commonly used in combination with other high blood pressure medications.

2)Chlorthalidone (Hygroton)

Thiazolidinedione drugs lower blood glucose by increasing the sensitivity of the cells to insulin (improving target cell response to insulin). Examples include pioglitazone (Actos) and rosiglitazone (Avandia)

Advantage:-

1)chlorthalidone is a prescription drug used to treat high blood pressure (hypertension).

2)Lowering high blood pressure helps prevent strokes, heart attacks, and kidney problems.
