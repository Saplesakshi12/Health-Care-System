Coronary artery disease

Generic and Trade Names of Drugs for Treatment of Coronary artery disease

1):- lisinopril (Prinivi):-

Lisinopril is an ACE inhibitor. ACE stands for angiotensin converting enzym.

Advantage:-

1)Lisinopril is used to treat high blood pressure (hypertension) in adults and children who are at least 6 years old. .

2)Lisinopril is also used to treat congestive heart failure in adults, or to improve survival after a heart attack.

Dosage:-

Applies to the following strengths: 10 mg; 2.5 mg; 5 mg; 20 mg; 40 mg; 30 mg; 1 mg/mL.

Side effect:-

cloudy urine
confusion
decrease in urine output or decrease in urine-concentrating ability

2)Amlodipine (Katerzia)

Amlodipine is a calcium channel blocker that dilates (widens) blood vessels and improves blood flow.

Advantage:-

1)Amlodipine is used to treat chest pain (angina) and other conditions caused by coronary artery disease.

2)Amlodipine is also used to treat high blood pressure (hypertension)

Dosage:-

Applies to the following strengths: 2.5 mg; 5 mg; 10 mg; 1 mg/mL.

Side effect:-

Difficult or labored breathing
dizziness
fast, irregular, pounding, or racing heartbeat or pulse
